Introduction

Welcome to Clinical Pathways Finder for Chronic Kidney Disease patients. This platform allows users to identify their expected clinical pathways by matching their profiles to past patients in our database. By entering your information from each visit, you will be able to see what patients similar to your profile have gone through in the past. Profile matching is performed using age, gender, and clinical attributes from each visit.

You will be able to see the expected pathways as a graph, linking one event to the next. Please note that it is not guranteed to be your pathway; you can always gain better health by working with your health providers for proper treatment and education.
